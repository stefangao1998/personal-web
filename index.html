About Me

Hi! I’m Stefan. I’m currently working as a Software Engineer at Servian, a IT consultanting company. I finished Honours degree of Software Engineering at UNSW. I’m from Ningbo, China. I came to Australia in 2015 and lived in Melbourne and Cairns for some time. Now I’ve been living in Sydney for 7 years.

My experience includes software development, data analysis, website implementation, mobile application development, cloud deployment, database operation. I have great interest and enthusiasm in cutting-edge technologies, such as NFT, ML, big data management, IoT and so on.

Personally, I am an energetic and passionate person with many interests and hobbies. I play saxophone and guitar in my spare time, Eric Clapton, Eagles and Kotaro Oshio are my favourite musicians, check out this YouTube video of me playing Eagles’ “I Can’t Tell You Why” electric guitar SOLO. Moreover, life lies in motion, road cycling, motorcycling and badminton are my favourite sports, and I'm learning bouldering recently. Feel free to contact me if you wanna join!

Life is full of challenges, I’m aiming to use my knowledge to satisfy different tasks and seek the opportunity to learn new things and gain experience.
I would love to hear your story, share my experience and learn about your challenges.
